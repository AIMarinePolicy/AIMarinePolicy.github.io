Dr. Henrique JB Marcos

Lead Researcher

Dr. Henrique Jerônimo Bezerra Marcos was born in Paraíba, Brazil. He holds two PhDs: one in Philosophy of Law from Maastricht University and another in International Law from São Paulo University. As a docent at the Foundations of Law Department, Maastricht University, his research utilizes philosophy to address conceptual and practical issues in international law, with a current focus on international decision-making in the climate crisis.

Dr. Rohan Nanda

Lead Researcher

Dr. Rohan Nanda is an Assistant Professor with a joint position at the Institute of Data Science and the Law & Tech Lab of Maastricht University. His research aims to develop and evaluate novel natural language processing technologies to tackle challenges in HR Analytics and Computational Law. He earned a Joint International Ph.D. in Law, Science and Technology, and a Ph.D. in Informatics from the University of Luxembourg and the University of Bologna.

Dr. Cristiana Teixeira Santos

External Partner

Dr. Cristiana Teixeira Santos is an Assistant Professor in Law and Technology at Utrecht University. She holds a joint international Doctoral Degree in Law, Science and Technology from the University of Bologna and a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Luxembourg. Her research focuses on privacy and data protection, artificial intelligence and law, and information technology law. She is also an expert for the Data Protection Unit at the Council of Europe and collaborates closely with computer scientists on online privacy.
